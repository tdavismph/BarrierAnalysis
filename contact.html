Training & Consulting Services

Learn about our available services and how to request support

How can I request a Barrier Analysis training for my organization?

To request a training for your organization, please fill out the contact form above and select "Training Request" as the inquiry type. Please include the following details in your message:

  • Approximate number of participants
  • Preferred training format (in-person or virtual)
  • Preferred dates or time frame
  • Specific sectors or behaviors of interest
  • Previous experience with Barrier Analysis (if any)

Our training team will respond with availability, customization options, and pricing information.

What technical assistance services do you offer?

We offer a range of technical assistance services to support organizations implementing Barrier Analysis, including:

  • Questionnaire design and adaptation
  • Study design and sampling strategy
  • Data analysis and interpretation support
  • Guidance on translating findings into program activities
  • Mentoring during implementation
  • Quality assurance review

Technical assistance can be provided remotely or in-person, depending on your needs and location.

Can you conduct a complete Barrier Analysis study for our organization?

Yes, we can provide end-to-end implementation of Barrier Analysis studies, from design through analysis and reporting. Our experienced team can conduct studies across various sectors and contexts, working closely with your organization to ensure that findings directly inform your programming.

For full implementation requests, please contact us with details about your project, target behaviors, location, and timeline. We'll provide a customized proposal based on your specific needs.
